The Business Enviroment of Tech World..


Interesting subject we're studying this year.. So they're not just teaching us how to become tech nerds.. they're theaching us how to become business nerds as well..


That's good.. You never know when you wake up one day and decide to open your own tech company.. because let's be serious.. we all think that Bill Gates could use some "friendly" competition


By studying "Business Enviroment" we learn that "Business Enviroment" is the combination of internal and external factors that influence a company's operating situation..

The business enviroment can include factors such as:

  • clients and suppliers
  • its competition and owners
  • improvements in technology
  • laws and gouverment activities
  • market, social and economic trends

So if you dream that someday you will have your own tech company.. study hard.. and study "Business Enviroment".. it helps..
